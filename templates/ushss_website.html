About USHSS

A Legacy of Intellectual Excellence

The University School of Humanities and Social Sciences (USHSS) is one of the founding schools of Guru Gobind Singh Indraprastha University, established in 1998 under the Government of NCT of Delhi.


Our school offers a vibrant academic environment that integrates rigorous scholarship with contemporary relevance — preparing students to engage deeply with the complex social, cultural, and philosophical questions of our time.

Scholarship & Inquiry

Research Focus Areas

Gender, Society & Culture

Examining the interplay of gender, caste, class and culture in contemporary Indian society through interdisciplinary lenses.

Political Economy & Development

Research on economic inequality, public policy, urban development, and inclusive growth in the Indian subcontinent.

Literature, Identity & Diaspora

Exploring how literary texts construct and challenge notions of national identity, belonging, and diaspora experience.

Mental Health & Wellbeing

Community-level studies on psychological wellbeing, stigma, counselling interventions and policy implications.

Constitutional Democracy & Governance

Analysing evolving democratic institutions, judicial review, and federal structure in India's political system.

Environmental Humanities

Cross-disciplinary work on ecology, climate justice, environmental ethics and human-nature relationships.
